About ASL Detection

This application leverages the power of machine learning to recognize American Sign Language (ASL) hand signs in real-time. It can detect every letter from A to Z, as well as the space symbol, making communication smoother for the hearing impaired.

Our model is trained on the "American_Sign_Language_Letters_Multiclass" dataset, ensuring accurate and reliable interpretation of ASL gestures. This dataset consists of thousands of hand sign images, allowing our AI to learn and understand subtle differences in ASL letters.

We believe in breaking down communication barriers and making technology accessible for everyone. This project is a step toward fostering a more inclusive society, bridging the gap between those who use ASL and those who don't.
